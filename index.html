I'm currently working in Accenture Technology Labs, Bangalore along with Dr. Anurag Dwarakanath as a Researcher on application of machine learning techniques on problems related to natural language texts. Prior to joining Accenture Technology Labs, I did my Master of Science by Research in Computer Science from IIIT Hyderabad under the guidance of Dr. Bruhadeshwar Bezawada. My Master's thesis was on Privacy Preserving String Pattern Matching on Outsourced Data using Searchable Encryption. Apart from Searchable Encryption, I am also interested in working on general problems related to the fields of applied cryptography and data mining. I have also created a brief literature survey on Privacy Preserving Machine Learning.
