I'm currently doing PhD in Computer Science at School of Engineering and Applied Science, University of Virginia. I am advised by Professor David Evans and my area of research is broadly in Privacy Preserving Machine Learning. Before beginning my doctoral studies, I was working in Accenture Technology Labs, Bangalore as a researcher on application of machine learning on problems related to software engineering. I have done my Master of Science by Research in Computer Science from IIIT Hyderabad where my Master's thesis was on Privacy Preserving String Pattern Matching on Outsourced Data using Searchable Encryption (under the guidance of Dr. Bruhadeshwar Bezawada).
